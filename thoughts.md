# Micro-Fencing

## Inputs
### Button
- 1 pin from PIC (PORT C)

### Accelerometer
- I2C Burst-Mode Communication
  - 2 pins from PIC (PORT C)

## Outputs
### Speaker
- 1 pin from PIC (PORT A)

### Light(s)
- LED Jewels
  - Tri-Color LEDs
  - 3 pins from PIC (PORT A)
- LED Strips (?)
  - Assume Tri-Color + Brightness
    - Connect color to Jewel bus (PORT A)
  - Use Port B?
